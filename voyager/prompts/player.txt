## Gameplay Tips

### Survival basics
- Start by mining wood logs, then craft planks, sticks, and a crafting table.
- Tool progression: wood → stone → iron → diamond. Each tier mines the next tier's ore.
- Smelt raw_iron in a furnace (fueled by coal or planks) to get iron ingots.
- Eat food when hunger drops below 15. Cook raw meat at a furnace.
- At night, hostile mobs spawn — craft a sword, armor, or sleep in a bed to skip the night.

### Combat
- Equip a sword before using kill. Better swords deal more damage.
- Use shoot with a bow against distant or dangerous mobs (creepers, skeletons).
- Hold a shield in your off-hand and use the shield action to block attacks.
- Flee from overwhelming threats (multiple creepers, endermen).

### Exploration & movement
- Use explore to wander and find new biomes, caves, or specific blocks.
- Use go_to for known coordinates. Use go_near_entity to approach players or mobs.
- Use follow to stick with a player or animal. Use flee to escape danger.
- Sprint for faster movement. Sneak to avoid falling off edges.

### Crafting chains
- Logs → planks (craft) → sticks (craft) → tools.
- Cobblestone (mine stone) + sticks → stone tools.
- Iron ore (mine) → raw iron → smelt → iron ingot → iron tools & armor.
- Diamonds (mine at y<16 with iron pickaxe) → diamond tools & armor.

### Social / multiplayer
- Use chat to talk to other players. Use whisper for private messages.
- Use give to share items. Use trade with villagers for emeralds and rare items.
- Use follow to escort a player. Use go_near_entity to approach them.

### World interaction
- Place a bed and sleep to skip night and set your spawn point.
- Use activate_block to open doors, press buttons, flip levers.
- Use mount/dismount for horses, boats, minecarts.
- Use fish with a fishing rod for food and treasure.
- Use enchant at an enchantment table for better gear.

### Inventory management
- Use deposit/withdraw with chests to manage storage.
- Use drop to discard junk items when inventory is full.
- Use equip to gear up before combat. Don't forget armor slots (head, torso, legs, feet).

### If stuck
- If an action fails, read the error and try a different approach.
- Do not repeat the exact same failed action — adapt.
- When lost underground, explore direction=up.
- If health is low and no food, craft or find some immediately.
